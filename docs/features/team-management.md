# Team Management

## Overview

Effective organization is key to attendance management. Presence allows you to structure your workforce into departments and teams, making it easier to monitor attendance and generate relevant reports.

## Features

- **Departments**: Group employees by function (e.g., Engineering, HR).
- **Employee Profiles**: Store essential information like contact details and roles.
- **Role-Based Access**: Assign Admin, Manager, or Employee roles.

## Managing Departments

### Creating a Department
1. Go to **Dashboard** → **Departments**.
2. Click **Add Department**.
3. Enter the **Department Name**.
4. (Optional) Assign a **Department Head**.
5. Click **Save**.

### Assigning Employees
You can assign employees to a department when creating their profile or editing it later.

## Employee Management

### Adding an Employee
1. Go to **Dashboard** → **Employees** or **Team**.
2. Click **Add Employee**.
3. Enter **Full Name**, **Email**, and **Job Title**.
4. Select a **Department** and **Role**.
5. Click **Create Profile**.

The employee will receive an invitation email to set up their password.

### User Roles

- **Admin**: Full system access. Can configure settings, add users, and view all reports.
- **Manager**: Can view attendance and approve leaves for their department/team.
- **Employee**: Can checking/out, request leave, and view their own history.
