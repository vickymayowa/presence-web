# Attendance Tracking

## Overview

The attendance tracking feature is the core of Presence. It allows employees to check in and out, tracks their working hours, and provides real-time visibility to managers and administrators.

## How to Check In

### Web Portal

1. Navigate to the dashboard
2. Click the "Check In" button
3. Optionally, allow camera access for facial verification
4. Confirm your check-in

### Mobile App

1. Open the Presence app
2. Tap the large "Check In" button
3. Follow the prompts for verification
4. You'll see a confirmation message

## Check-In Rules

### Time Windows

Administrators can configure specific time windows during which check-in is allowed. If you try to check in outside these windows, you'll see a message indicating when the next window opens.

### Location Requirements

Some organizations may require location verification. If enabled, ensure:
- Location services are enabled on your device
- You're within the designated check-in area

### Verification Methods

Presence supports multiple verification methods:
- **Password-based**: Simple username/password
- **Facial Recognition**: AI-powered identity verification
- **NFC Card**: Tap your ID card to check in
- **Beacon Proximity**: Automatic check-in when near office beacons

## Viewing Attendance

### Personal Attendance

Employees can view their attendance history:
1. Go to **Dashboard** → **Attendance**
2. See your recent check-ins/check-outs
3. Filter by date range
4. Export your records if needed

### Team Attendance (Managers)

Managers can monitor their team's attendance:
1. Go to **Dashboard** → **Team**
2. View real-time status of all team members
3. See who's present, absent, or late
4. Filter and sort by various criteria

### Company-Wide Attendance (HR/CEO)

HR and CEO roles have full visibility:
1. Go to **Dashboard** → **Reports**
2. View attendance across all departments
3. Generate custom reports
4. Export data for payroll integration

## Common Issues

### Q: I can't check in. Why?

**A:** Common reasons include:
- You're outside the allowed check-in window
- You're not within the required location
- Your account doesn't have check-in permissions
- Network connectivity issues

### Q: I forgot to check out. What should I do?

**A:** Contact your manager or HR administrator. They can manually adjust your check-out time.

### Q: Can I check in from home?

**A:** This depends on your organization's policies. Some allow remote check-in, others require on-site verification.

## Best Practices

1. **Be Punctual**: Check in within the designated time window
2. **Enable Notifications**: Get reminders to check in/out
3. **Review Regularly**: Check your attendance history weekly
4. **Report Issues Immediately**: If you notice any errors, report them right away
5. **Keep App Updated**: Always use the latest version of the mobile app
