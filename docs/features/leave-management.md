# Leave Management

## Overview

Presence simplifies the process of managing employee time off. Employees can request leaves, view their balances, and track approval status, while managers can review and approve requests efficiently.

## Features

- **Multiple Leave Types**: Support for Annual, Sick, Casual, and Unpaid leave.
- **Balance Tracking**: Automatic calculation of remaining leave days.
- **Approval Workflow**: Managers get notified of new requests and can approve or reject them.
- **Calendar View**: Visual overview of team availability.

## How to Request Leave (Employees)

1. Navigate to **Dashboard** → **Leaves**.
2. Click **Request Leave**.
3. Select the **Leave Type** (e.g., Sick Leave).
4. Choose the **Start Date** and **End Date**.
5. Add a **Reason** (optional but recommended).
6. Click **Submit**.

You will receive a notification when your manager takes action on your request.

## How to Manage Requests (Managers)

1. Navigate to **Dashboard** → **Approvals** or **Leaves**.
2. View the list of **Pending Requests**.
3. Click on a request to see details.
4. Click **Approve** or **Reject**.
5. Optionally add a comment explaining your decision.

## Leave Types

- **Annual Leave**: Standard paid time off.
- **Sick Leave**: For medical reasons.
- **Casual Leave**: Short-term personal leave.
- **Maternity/Paternity**: Parental leave as per policy.
- **Unpaid Leave**: Time off without pay.

## Leave Balances

Balances are typically reset at the beginning of the year or based on the employee's joining date. You can view your current balance at the top of the Leaves dashboard.
